    This page is designed to approximate the water volume for different aquarium shapes and system arrangements. The net water volume considers water displaced by live rock weighted wet and sand weighted dry.
    Make your measurements from the inside to account for the panes thickness.
    If your tank walls are tapered (not vertical) try to measure the width and/or side at the mid distance between the bottom and the water surface.
    Your estimate for water volume inside pipes and purification equipment can be added as other volume.
